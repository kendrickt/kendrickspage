What's this site all about?

In Dungeons & Dragons, fifty feet of rope come in the standard Adventurer's Pack. While it's such a simple and ordinary piece of equipment, it's utility is limited only by imagination (and the Dungeon Master). I want to apply that idea to the other tools I have in real life! Fifty Feet of Rope is a way for me to share my personal adventure as I explore different ways of using my skills.

On the home page, I'll feature the most current project. Usually, this will be a project that I'm working on, but it could potentially be a something else (e.g. a movie that's changed my life, or a new musician I can't stop listening to). There's also space for a few more items to share. On the projects page, I'll have an assortment of projects I've worked on. This will include data analysis/data visualization, writing, photographs, and more!

More about me!

I'm 23 years old, which makes me the youngest adult in my family. This section will grow as I grow, but for now I'll just list some interesting things about myself:

  • I've been in zero gravity! I was able to fly in NASA's Weightless Wonder through their Microgravity University Program.
  • In high school, I pole vaulted! My personal record was 12' 6". I also long jumped, and my recorded personal record is 12', which is hilariously bad.
  • I've spent quite a bit of time travelling in southeast Asia, and was actually in Nepal a few weeks before the 2015 earthquakes.
