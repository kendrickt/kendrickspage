January 3rd, 2016

I took a short hiatus to transfer my blog posts from Google's blog application to this website. I had to learn a few new technologies, but now that that's been sorted out I'm back in business! Before I move away from home field advantages, I transfered my local system into a little web application!

Just few quick notes for the user:

  • PPG = Points Per Game
  • PAPG = Points Allowed Per Game
  • SPG = Spread Per Game
  • When not specified, the stat is the at-home statistic, e.g. PPG means points per game, while at home.
  • The ratio of a stat is the at-home to away ratio.
  • The diff of a stat is the at-home stat minus the away stat.
